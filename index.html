About Us

Tithandizane Community Trust is a non-governmental organization dedicated to improving the lives of people in our community. Our name, "Tithandizane," means "Let's help each other" in Chichewa, reflecting our commitment to community support and empowerment.

Our Programs

  • Water and sanitation projects
  • Education support for underprivileged children
  • Healthcare initiatives for rural areas
  • Sustainable agriculture projects
  • Women's empowerment programs
